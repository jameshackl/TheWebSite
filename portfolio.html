Crypto-Currency Arbitrage


  Crypto currencies are a really neat and novel invention. Just the amount of forethought and security baked into them is impressive. I don't claim to know all their is to know about them or the intricacies of various trading straegies but arbitrage is one that is mathematically defineable. Arbitrage is simply taking advantage price inefficiencies. Someone is selling too low or buying too high.

  This project compiles data from several crypto currency exchange's REST APIs in order to find all the arbitrage opportunities. Further screening is done to determine which ones are actually possible, meet minimum transfer sizes, more profitable than the fees incurred, and apply blacklists. The result is an ordered set of transactions that when made in order and at an acceptable price will be profitable.

  Currently, It can opperate as an analysis tool and the transactions must be made manually. There are many market variables to account for yet in automating it but it is on the TODO list.
